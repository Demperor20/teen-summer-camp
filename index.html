About Teen Summer Camp

Teen Summer Camp is a program under Ecwa Goodnews Church children section aim to train children in the way of the lord during their school breaks so as to engage them with sound teaching both morally and spiritually and above all also equip them with basic skills that can be very useful to them in this present country situation.

What You'll Learn

Sound Bible Studies Infomation Technology Entrepreneurship and Food Expo Games and Sports Networking Sessions
